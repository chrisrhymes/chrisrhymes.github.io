We rejoin David and Evelyn after the dramatic events in Detained at the Tower, with David in hospital and Evelyn keeping a close eye on him.

DI Barksley is forced onto another case, this time at the Estate, investigating reports of an intruder.

George being George, has his theory and won't wait to investigate and volunteers to research the mystery of Little-Astwick in his own special way.

However, things quickly unravel with George injured and Barksley missing, leaving David and Evelyn no choice but to continue the investigation, that all seems to centre around the Estate, just outside the village of Little-Astwick.

The more they uncover, the more there is for them to lose. David and Evelyn rejoin their new friend, forensic officer Danielle Thompson, at the estate where they must race to solve the mystery and rescue their friends in The chase at the Estate!
